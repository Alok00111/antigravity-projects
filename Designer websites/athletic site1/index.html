The V-Shape Blueprint

Latissimus Dorsi

THE WINGS

The broadest muscle of the back. Creates the dramatic V-taper that defines an athletic silhouette. Responsible for shoulder adduction and extension.

WIDTH Primary Impact
Pull-ups Key Exercise

Lateral Deltoids

THE CAPS

The middle head of the deltoid muscle. Provides the capped, rounded shoulder look that widens the frame and exaggerates the V-taper from the front.

BREADTH Primary Impact
Lateral Raise Key Exercise

Anterior Deltoids

THE FRONT SHELF

The front head of the shoulder. Contributes to the 3D rounded look and is critical for pressing movements and front-facing aesthetics.

DEPTH Primary Impact
OHP Key Exercise

Trapezius

THE YOKE

A large diamond-shaped muscle spanning the upper back to the neck. Creates the powerful, thick look that conveys raw strength and stability.

POWER Primary Impact
Shrugs Key Exercise

Pectoralis Major

THE PLATES

The large fan-shaped chest muscle. Well-developed pecs create the shelf-like upper body that commands attention and balances the V-taper from the front.

MASS Primary Impact
Bench Press Key Exercise

External Obliques

THE FRAME

The muscles running diagonally along the sides of the abdomen. Create the chiseled, tapered waistline that accentuates the V-shape from every angle.

TAPER Primary Impact
Cable Twist Key Exercise
